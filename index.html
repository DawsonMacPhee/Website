I graduated in 2019 with Principal's honour roll and moved onto an undergratuate in a Bachelor of Computing program at the University of Guelph.

In grade nine I decided I wanted to become a software developer, and imediatly started to teach myself to code. This earned me a Certificate of Distinction with the Center for Mathematics and Computing at the University of Waterloo. Now in University, I am working to refine my stuctures and usage of the languages to become as knowlageable as I can in my field.
